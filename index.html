About
Security-first. Research-driven.

I am an Information Security professional at India Post Payments Bank (IPPB), where I work as an Assistant Manager (IT) specialising in Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing (VAPT) and Network Intrusion Prevention Systems (NIPS).

My role involves identifying and mitigating vulnerabilities across banking systems, monitoring and blocking malicious network activity in real time, and contributing to IPPB's cybersecurity posture in alignment with RBI cybersecurity guidelines and CERT-In advisories.

Academically, I hold an M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Dharwad, with a strong research foundation in computer architecture, deep learning systems, and parallel computing. My thesis investigates the Polynomial Mixer (PoM) as a linear-time replacement for self-attention in MambaVision — a state-of-the-art hybrid vision backbone.

My long-term vision is to grow into a senior Information Security leadership role within the banking and financial services sector — contributing to strategic security governance, risk management, and cyber resilience.

M.Tech Thesis · IIT Dharwad · Jan 2026 – Present
MambaVision + PoM: Polynomial Mixing as a drop-in attention replacement in a hybrid Mamba-Transformer vision backbone
Investigating whether the Polynomial Mixer (PoM), a linear-time token-mixing operator from CVPR 2026, can replace self-attention in MambaVision (CVPR 2025, NVIDIA) while preserving accuracy and improving efficiency at high resolution. The core finding: PoM delivers a theory-consistent throughput advantage in the long-sequence regime, while memory remains identical due to PyTorch's Flash-Attention-compatible SDPA kernel.
